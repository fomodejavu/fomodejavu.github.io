The power of
compounding
over time

Time in the market beats timing the market. A single consistent investment, held patiently, grows geometrically — not linearly. The table shows $1,000 growing at the S&P 500's historical ~10% average annual return.

The uncomfortable truth: most of the final wealth is created in the last few years, not the first. This is why starting early is the single most powerful investing decision you can make.
